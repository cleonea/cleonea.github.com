I had never given much thought to how would my life end. Death was my companion for a long time, a common experience. I took granted that every day could be my last, yet I had no idea what would count as a good way to go. I knew very well how it felt to be left over and over again, but I had never imagined myself leaving.

Then, one day, me and my fate bumped into each other. I wasn’t warned, wasn’t given a chance to say my goodbyes, wasn’t even allowed to remember. My life, as I knew it, faded into thin air. Which is funny, for air used to be paramount.

Was it a good way to go? No. Was it a way at all? Not even.

Now, there is only one question to be answered. Had you find your life, your everything taken away, would you dare to start it all over again?
